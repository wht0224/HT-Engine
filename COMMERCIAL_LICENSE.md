### Student Teams Discount

Student development teams where all members are current students holding valid student ID can enjoy a 50% discount on all team and enterprise annual fee tiers (Small Team, Medium Team, Large Enterprise).

- Eligible groups: Teams exclusively composed of students
- Applicable tiers: Small Team, Medium Team, Large Enterprise annual fees
- Discount: 50% off
- Application: Submit valid student ID copies for all members and a team roster by email (13910593150@139.com)

This discount also applies to users granted such benefit according to LICENSE or prior agreements. If terms change in the future, already purchased licenses are not affected.
